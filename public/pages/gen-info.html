GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT OUR PRODUCTS

Wewe Import is a commercial fashion website selling handmade products from Kenya. Beautiful leather beaded sandals inspired by the work of the Masai. We also sell nice Kikois and Kikois towels as well as leather beaded dog collars. Look at our various accessories for belts, kikois buckles, key chains and a small selection of handbags to come.

About our products

The sandals and leather products are handmade in Kenya. All our products are of excellent quality leather, creative bead work designs that are timeless. Handmade brass buckles and accessories.

Sizes for Sandals

The sandals are stamped with European sizes. Women sizes run from 35 to 41. A size 41 is the equivalent of an American size 10. Children sizes run from Size 26 to 34.
Men sizes run from Size 41 to 46.

Here is the equivalent for the rest of the world.

European Size 37 = US 6.5 = UK 4
