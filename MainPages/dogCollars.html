DOG COLLARS - STYLE SHEET

Order Online

Excellent quality and most unique beaded dog collars with handmade brass buckles. The styles from the belts and the dog collars are the same, so are the style names. They are available in 5 sizes from 8 to 24 inches and only 1 lenght for the dog leads. The beading is done by Maasai women working in their home area. The artisans can earn a living while maintaining their traditional lifestyles.
